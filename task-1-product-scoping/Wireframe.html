v1 Scoping Notes — Wireframe Annotations

  • Brand selector: Multi-client from day one. Each brand maps to its own /data/[brand]/ CSV folder.
  • Focus highlight: Computed as biggest absolute WoW delta across all channels. Logic is simple and visible — no black box.
  • WoW delta: (this_week − last_week) / last_week × 100. Same formula every time. Defined once, used everywhere.
  • Status badges (On track / Watch / Underperforming): Threshold-based. Default: >+10% = On track, <−10% = Underperforming. Configurable per brand in v2.
  • "Trends" and "All brands" tabs: Visible but disabled. Shows users what's coming without hiding v1's intentional scope.
  • No login: Internal tool. Shared intranet link. Auth is a v2 concern when client-facing access is needed.
  • Missing data card: Shows explicitly when a channel has no data for the week. Never silently shows zero or stale data.
