Wireframe Notes (Scoping Decisions)

  • Brand selector: Multi-client from day one. Each brand maps to its own CSV folder — /data/[brand]/
  • Focus highlight: Biggest absolute week-on-week delta across channels. Simple rule, no black box.
  • WoW delta formula: (this week − last week) / last week × 100. Same formula every time.
  • Status badges: Threshold-based. Above +10% = On track. Below −10% = Underperforming. In between = Watch.
  • V2 tabs visible but disabled: Shows what's coming without pretending it's built.
  • No login required: Internal tool on shared intranet link. Auth is a v2 concern.
  • Missing data: Shows "No data this week" card explicitly. Never shows zero or stale data silently.
